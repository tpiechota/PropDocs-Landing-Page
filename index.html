How It Works

Step 1

Simply log in and populate the new tenant and property information form any device at any time.

Step 2

Lease and other PropDocs will be automatically generated and sent to the tenant for electronic signature.

Step 3

Tenant verifies, signs and returns the PropDocs digitally on any device at any time.

Step 4

Your online dashboard keeps track of the status of all your PropDocs - sent, delivered, signed, received.

Step 5

System follows up with any outstanding documents and reminders by email/SMS thereby removing associated hassle.

FAQ’s

Is there a catch?

Nope, no funny business. All it takes to begin using our PropDocs feature is your email address and a few other pieces of information outlined above and you’ll enjoy FULL access for a month. Use anytime, cancel anytime.

Are there any limits?

After a free month of full access, you can decide if you would like to continue using the service for a monthly subscription of just £20 (€30). You can also decide to pay up-front for a full year subscription at a 25% reduced rate. There will never be any cancellation fees.

When and how do I get paid my commission?

We pay you a commision every two months. You can choose how you would like to receive it specifically (i.e. per agent or in total)

How do Property Button make money?

Property Button are the largest channel to market for the major gas, electricity and media suppliers in the UK & Ireland who pay us for providing the service to our wonderful agents.

Are there any added costs to tenants?

Nope. Never was, never is, never will be.

Do I need to download any software?

There are no software or installation requirements. It couldn't be easier, Property Button is an online portal and so all you need is your login details.

Do I need training?

Our portal is very user friendly but Property Button will guide you through the process over the phone or in person if needed.

Will the tenant have a choice of supplier?

Of course! The tenant can choose any supplier and service they want, Property Button will give the tenant the information needed to make an informed decision that suits their needs.

Are we compliant with data-protection laws?

Yes Property Button are fully compliant with european data protection law and and also the commissioner of energy regulations (UK & Ireland).

What do you do with the property and tenant information?

We will not do a thing without prior consent from you and the tenant. Once we receive tenant consent, we will use their information to identify what services are currently available at the property using the information provided.
