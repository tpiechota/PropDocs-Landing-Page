How It Works

Step 1

Simply log in and populate the new tenant and property information form any device at any time.

Step 2

Lease and other PropDocs will be automatically generated and sent to the tenant for electronic signature.

Step 3

Tenant verifies, signs and returns the PropDocs digitally on any device at any time.

Step 4

Your online dashboard keeps track of the status of all your PropDocs - sent, delivered, signed, received.

Step 5

System follows up with any outstanding documents and reminders by email/SMS thereby removing associated hassle.
